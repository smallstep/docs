The right provisioner depends on your operational environment.

<p>
  The JWK provisioner is the most general-purpose provisioner. It supports password and one-time token-based authentication. To add a JWK provisioner called{' '}
  <inlineCode>{props.doc.slug}</inlineCode> to a hosted Certificate Manager
  authority (if you haven't already), run:
</p>

<CodeBlock autoCopy language="shell">
  {`step beta ca provisioner add ${props.doc.slug} --type JWK --create --x509-default-dur 720h`}
</CodeBlock>

For instructions on adding provisioners to open source `step-ca`, or to learn more about other provisioner types, see _[Configuring `step-ca` Provisioners](https://smallstep.com/docs/step-ca/provisioners)_.
